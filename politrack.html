PoliTrack - A platform for tracking political data

PoliTrack is a mobile app I designed in Figma with a team of colleagues to make Portuguese parliamentary activity more transparent and accessible to everyone. The app allows users to explore political initiatives by party, see how each one voted, access official documents, and read each party's election manifesto. Users can also "approve" or "reject" initiatives, and future features will include comments and public discussion. I redesigned the entire visual identity of the app — including the logo, icon, and branding — with a modern and accessible approach aimed at both younger audiences (inspired by platforms like X/Twitter) and older generations (similar to Facebook), while giving the platform a unique visual language and interaction style. I was fully responsible for the prototyping of the entire user experience in Figma, including all user flows and interactions. I'm also involved in communication and social media for the project. This project combines UI/UX, branding, and communication strategy to help create a more informed and democratic Portugal.
