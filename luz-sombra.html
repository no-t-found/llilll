“6” | Day & Night | Editorial design

What does it feel like to live 6 months in constant daylight… and then 6 months in uninterrupted darkness? This 2024 editorial project explores the unique emotional and geographical experience of those living near the Earth’s poles. The book navigates the duality of light and shadow, using illustration as a poetic tool to reflect isolation, rhythm, and inner transformation. Inspired by the quote: “Chega um momento que não tem mais dia. Só um ‘lusco-fusco’, e traz-me a sensação de solidão.” — Nathália Pimenta. Visually minimal and concept-driven, this book blends emotional storytelling and poetry with geographical symbolism, showing how the extremes of nature can mirror our internal states.
